Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login times, used solely for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide exclusively to operate Spendly:

  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Display your expenses and generate spending summaries.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces for the interface. No personal data is sent.
  • Hosting provider — stores and serves the application. Bound by a data processing agreement.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts are embedded in Spendly.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
